JLI LinkedIn Declutter

A local-only Chrome extension that hides and collapses distracting LinkedIn feed and notification clutter.

Single Purpose

JLI LinkedIn Declutter locally hides and collapses distracting LinkedIn feed and notification clutter so users can browse LinkedIn with less noise.

What It Does

  • Hides LinkedIn News and right-rail clutter.
  • Hides right-rail ad iframes.
  • Collapses promoted, sponsored, and suggested feed posts.
  • Hides job recommendation feed cards.
  • Collapses recommended follow modules.
  • Collapses reaction and analytics notifications.

What It Does Not Do

  • Does not use LinkedIn APIs.
  • Does not send messages or post content.
  • Does not click buttons, like posts, follow people, or connect with people.
  • Does not load remote code.
  • Does not transmit data to the developer or external servers.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

The extension processes LinkedIn page content locally and temporarily in the browser to identify clutter, such as promoted posts, sponsored content, suggested posts, recommendation modules, reaction notifications, and analytics notifications. It does not store, transmit, sell, or share LinkedIn website content.

The extension stores only local configuration settings, such as whether feed cleanup and notification cleanup are enabled. Settings are stored with Chrome extension storage on your device under jliDeclutterConfig.

No analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, remote logging, or remote code are included. All JavaScript, CSS, and assets are packaged with the extension.

Permissions

Storage: Used only to save local extension settings, such as enabled cleanup features. These settings are not transmitted to the developer or any external server.

LinkedIn host access: Required for https://www.linkedin.com/* so the extension can run on LinkedIn pages, inspect page content locally, and hide or collapse matching clutter. Access is limited to LinkedIn.

Deleting Data

You can reset settings from the extension options page or remove the extension from Chrome to delete its local extension storage.
