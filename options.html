JLI Declutter

Local LinkedIn decluttering for feed and notification noise.

What this extension does

The packaged extension runs a local content script on LinkedIn. It reads visible page text to classify cards, collapses distracting items, watches for newly loaded items, and sends no data anywhere.

Included rules

  • Collapse promoted/sponsored-looking feed cards.
  • Collapse social/reaction feed cards.
  • Hide job recommendation carousels.
  • Collapse reaction and analytics notifications.

Collapsed cards expand on hover.

Customize what gets collapsed

Click the extension icon in the toolbar to toggle individual categories on or off. All settings are stored locally via chrome.storage.sync.

Debug tip

If a card is missed, LinkedIn probably changed its markup or wording. The desktop runtime favors small detection-pattern updates over broad structural selectors.
